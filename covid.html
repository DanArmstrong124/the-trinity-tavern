COVID UPDATE

What has changed?

One Meter

We've worked hard to get our pub to meet Covid guidelines, we need you to respect social distancing to allow us to keep our doors open in this tough time.

Seating

We have added more seating to our outside area, along with a great refurb with grass area and fencing to provide a safer environment for kids and dogs.

Sanitise

You will find sanitiser stations at each entrance and exit and throughout the pub. To keep safe, we ask that you utilise our stations before entering and leaving our Inn.

Emergency Contact

For your safety, your name and telephone number will be taken for track and trace purposes.

Card Only

Card payments only please to avoid risk of transference of virus with cash.

Covid Training

Staff are fully trained in new health and safety regulations for COVID, enforcing social distancing and noting down whether any of them have shown COVID symptoms when signing into work.

One Way Dining

One way system when ordering and collecting drinks and a strict one at a time rule for in the toilets.

Coming To You

Table service outside can be provided if a customer requires this, we also have an online order and payment app to support this.
